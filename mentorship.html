Mentorship

Connecting Columbia students with inspirational professionals

As a collegiate space embedded within the nationwide Lean In network, our club is uniquely situated to forge connections between college and industry. With our mentorship program, we make the most of that special position. The program, starting October 2017, pairs our Lean In members with inspirational female professionals who are passionate about promoting women at work

Details

Commitment: mid Oct - April, biweekly meetings.

Mentors across disciplines, including consulting, finance, tech.

Since we have limited spots in our program, active Lean In circle members will be given priority as mentees.
