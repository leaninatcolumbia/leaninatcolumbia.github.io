Lean In at Columbia University

Empowering women of all identities and disciplines to lean into their lives

Lean In is an international organization, founded by Sheryl Sandberg, the COO of Facebook, which promotes the success of women in the workplace. All over the globe, chapters of Lean In organize events, programming, and circles, groups of 10-12 individuals who meet weekly to discuss both personal and professional goals. 


Lean In @ Columbia, the official chapter of Lean In at Columbia University, is a community like no other on campus. Our trained circle moderators impart valuable practical skills and foster discussion and debate. Our diverse and passionate members, spanning all disciplines, encourage one another to make the most of their college experience and to pursue fulfilling careers.

"It's time to cheer on girls and women who want to sit at the table"
-Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook and Founder of Lean In
