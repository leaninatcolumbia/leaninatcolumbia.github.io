So who are we?
The simple answer: we're a chapter of LeanIn.Org, a registered 501(c)(3) organization founded by Sheryl Sandberg, the Chief Operating Officer of Facebook. Through our programming, we help our members develop both personally and professionally.
The better answer: we're a community like no other on campus, consisting of inspiring, brilliant, diverse women who support each other as they grow together.
